# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Domain experts (supply side) and hackathon judges evaluating the demo. Experts are mid-to-senior professionals who want high-fit paid demand without the spam of recruiting emails. Judges are technical evaluators watching a 90-second live voice interview and judging the result screen in real time. Both need to trust the product in under 10 seconds.

## Product Purpose

Confer is a voice-fed expert network: a voice agent interviews an expert, extracts a structured dossier, then surfaces high-fit paid demand and peer introductions. The durable asset is the expertise graph that compounds with every call. For the hackathon: one wow moment — the judge hears their own voice interview reflected back as a matched dossier.

## Brand Personality

Expert, precise, warm. The tone is a confident consulting firm that actually respects your time, not an AI hype product. Copy is direct; the system does the heavy lifting, not the UI.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI SaaS chat UI (ChatGPT clones, white/gray bubble interfaces)
- Dark SaaS dashboards in the Vercel/Linear/Raycast aesthetic — overused at hackathons
- Neon on black / crypto-AI hype visual language
- GLG/AlphaSights corporate enterprise blue

## Design Principles

1. The graph does the work, the UI gets out of the way.
2. Earned familiarity: standard affordances, no invented patterns.
3. Live state is the hero: transcript and results panels communicate what is happening right now.
4. Warm restraint: light, calm, expert-feeling — not cold, not loud.
5. One primary action per screen state: nothing competes with "Start conversation."

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Status changes conveyed via both color and text (the pill label, not just the dot). Screen reader-accessible status element. No motion for decorative purposes only.
